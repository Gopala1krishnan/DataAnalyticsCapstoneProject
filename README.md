# DataAnalyticsCapstoneProject
Purpose of this project is to support the need by quantifying ED burden as a ratio of encounters to available treatment stations, providing a standardized metric of demand relative to physical capacity. 
Lower ratios reflect fewer encounters per station and suggest less burden, while higher ratios indicate greater encounter pressure on each station and, potentially, higher operational strain. 
By expressing ED activity as encounters per treatment station, the dataset enables analysts and decision-makers to assess how heavily ED care spaces are utilized during a given time and to compare patterns 
across facilities, regions, and time
